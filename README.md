# Simple Mailing Form

A simple mailing form implemented using **Bootstrap**, **JavaScript**, **HTML**, and the **`mailto:`** protocol to open your default email client or mailing app.

## Preview

[Click here to deploy it on your device.](https://soligabiann.github.io/Mailing_Form/)

---

## Reminder

You may need to configure a default email application or service that handles the **`mailto:`** protocol in your device or browser settings.
